About

This website showcases work from the Data Journalism course at the Hertie School in Berlin (Spring 2026). Students produced data bits — short, focused data journalism pieces — and a final project over the course of the semester. The projects span investigative data work, interactive visualizations, and explanatory journalism. All featured projects are open source.

About the course

GRAD-E1493: Data Journalism is taught by Prof. Simon Munzert at the Hertie School, Berlin. Topics covered include web scraping, survey analysis, maps and geodata, data visualization, and interactive graphics.
